Frequently Asked Questions

What is the monthly cost of your app? +

The monthly cost of our app depends on the subscription plan you choose. We offer a Basic plan starting at $9.99 per month, a Standard plan at $19.99 per month, and a Premium plan at $29.99 per month. Each plan includes different features and access levels.

Do you have any local branches? +

Yes, we have local branches in several major cities including New York, London, Paris, and Sydney. For specific addresses and contact information, please visit the "Contact Us" section of our website.

What do I need to create an account? +

Creating an account is simple! You'll need to provide a valid email address, choose a unique username, and set a secure password. Once you've completed the registration form, check your email for a verification link to activate your account.

Do you have iOS and Android apps? +

Yes, we offer both iOS and Android apps! You can download our app from the Apple App Store for iOS devices and from Google Play for Android devices. Our mobile apps provide all the functionality of the web platform, optimized for smaller screens.

How do I create an account? +

To create an account, visit our sign-up page, enter your details (name, email, and password), and click "Sign Up." A verification email will be sent to you shortly. Click on the link in the email to confirm your registration, and you’re all set!
